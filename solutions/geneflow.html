GeneFlow

Overview

GeneFlow™ is an end-to-end bioinformatics platform for processing, analyzing, and visualizing genomic and proteomic datasets. It accelerates variant calling, expression analysis, proteome quantification, and systems-level interpretation. Target customers include Academic labs, biotech/pharma R&D, clinical genomics labs, precision medicine teams. Typical deployments involve Cloud-native pipelines with reproducible workflow managers, secure cohorts for federated analyses.

Capabilities

  • Raw data QC and preprocessing pipelines
  • Variant calling, annotation, and prioritization
  • RNA‑seq differential expression and fusion detection
  • Proteomics peptide identification and quantitation workflows
  • Multi-omics integration and pathway/network analysis
  • Interactive dashboards and reproducible analysis notebooks
