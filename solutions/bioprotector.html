BioProtector

Designed to satisfy privacy laws, clinical trial regulations, and trackability required by regulators; promotes safe, auditable AI adoption.

Overview

BioProtector™ provides security, privacy, and compliance tooling specifically for life-sciences and healthcare AI systems — protecting patient and research data while enabling compliant model development and deployment.

Capabilities

  • Data access governance, encryption, and key management
  • De-identification and synthetic data generation for sharing
  • Model risk assessment, bias/audit toolkits, and automated compliance reporting
  • Incident detection and response for data breaches and model misuse
