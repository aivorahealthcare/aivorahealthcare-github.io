GeneCure

GeneCure is a translational research and development platform focused on designing, optimizing, and validating gene therapies and genetic engineering constructs.

Overview

GeneCure™ features computational protein design, sequence optimization algorithms, off-target prediction ML models, laboratory automation integrations, LIMS and ELN connectivity. Typical deployment : Design → computational safety screening → bench validation → preclinical models → GMP transfer for clinical trials. Target customers include gene therapy startups, academic translational labs, contract development and manufacturing organizations, large biopharma. Strict adherence to biosafety, institutional approvals, GMP requirements for clinical manufacturing, transparency about risks and on/off-target effects, ethical review for germline edits.

Capabilities

  • In silico design of constructs - promoter selection, codon optimization, regulatory element tuning
  • gRNA design and off-target prediction for CRISPR systems
  • Vector optimization, capsid engineering, and manufacturability analytics
  • Preclinical in‑silico to in‑vitro screening pipelines and LIMS integration
  • Regulatory-ready documentation and batch tracking for GMP handoff
