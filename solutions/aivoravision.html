AivoraVision

Advanced medical imaging diagnostics powered by responsible AI .

Overview

AIvoraVision™ brings explainable medical image analysis for hospitals and research institutions that analyzes radiology and pathology images to detect, quantify, and prioritize abnormalities. It’s designed to augment workflows, and increase diagnostic sensitivity and specificity. Typical deployments include an edge device in imaging suite for rapid triage; cloud-hosted for enterprise analytics; hybrid deployments for data residency. Key technologies used deep convolutional neural networks, transformer-based vision models, containerized inference and model explainability modules.

Capabilities

  • Radiology image segmentation, classification and progression tracking
  • Real-time anomaly detection and multi-modality fusion
  • Explainable AI outputs - heatmaps, bounding boxes, confidence scores and structured reports
  • FHIR, DICOM, PACS, RIS and EMR compliant integrations
